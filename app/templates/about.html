NYUAD Market


NYUADmarket was developed by the NYU Abu Dhabi students in partial fulfillment of the course requirement of Software Engineering course.

NYUAD Market allows NYUAD students to conveniently trade goods. The seller can post items and interested parties are able to interact with him.

The site is linked to subscribers' nyu email addresses, which allows exclusivity of members and a good means of communication. No personal information is collected through this website.

Development team: Weichen Zhu, Talha Javed and Derara Hailegeorgis
